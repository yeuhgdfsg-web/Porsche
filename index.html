About Porsche

Porsche 911

The Porsche 911 is a timeless sports car icon, known for its rear-engine design, exceptional handling, and thrilling performance across all generations.

  • Top Speed: 293 km/h
  • Engine: Twin-Turbo 6-Cylinder
  • 0–100 km: 3.4 seconds

Porsche Taycan

Porsche Taycan is the brand’s first fully electric sports car, combining futuristic design with instant acceleration and zero-emission performance.

  • Turbocharged Electric Motors
  • Fast Charging up to 270 kW
  • Range up to 480 km

Porsche Cayenne

The Porsche Cayenne is a luxury SUV that blends performance, comfort, and everyday practicality while keeping Porsche’s sporty DNA.

  • Luxury Interior
  • Sport Mode Handling
  • V6 or V8 Engines
